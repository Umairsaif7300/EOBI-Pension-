EOBI Pension Information

Pension

EOBI provides monthly pension to retired workers who have contributed to the scheme during their employment period. The payment of pension depends on the monthly salary of the employee and the period of service.

Invalidity Pension

An Invalidity pension is also a financial help to the disabled person who becomes disabled for a lifetime due to illness or an accident during the service. This pension provides him with everyday life items like food, clothes, and many other things.

Survivor's Pension

EOBI provides financial support to the widow of the worker or other family members of the worker in case of an employee's death due to an accident or illness. This pension is provided monthly to cover their daily life expenses.

Old-Age Grant

When an insured person cannot qualify for the monthly pension due to insufficient contribution, EOBI gives a one-time lump sum payment known as the Old-Age Grant.

How to Claim EOBI Pension?

  1. EOBI Registration: Employers are responsible for enrolling their employees and deducting contributions from their salaries.
  2. Contribution Requirements: Both employers and employees contribute a percentage of the employee's salary.
  3. Necessary Documents: Employees need to submit relevant documents like national identity cards and employment records.
  4. Pension Application: Submit a pension application with relevant documents for approval.
  5. Verification: EOBI verifies eligibility for the pension.
  6. Disbursement: Once approved, EOBI provides monthly pension or a lump sum amount.
